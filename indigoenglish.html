What is Indigo?

Indigo is the most complete application to structure, accelerate and refocus knowledge in children and young people with severe learning problems, ADHD, Asperger and Autism in general (ASD) that allows to reconnect the cerebral areas and simplify any learning process. Indigo is the ideal tool for parents, psychologists, psychiatrists, teachers and therapists.

For a future without limitations

We believe that every child is special and that education gives them the best opportunity to live life to the fullest. That's why our Indigo software makes resources of the highest quality available to all children and young people to help them reach their maximum learning potential.

What goals can a child achieve by using Indigo properly and constantly?

  • Learn to concentrate
  • Learn to take into account the details
  • Learn to solve problems and make decisions
  • Learn to cope with everyday situations
  • Learn to read (even in other languages)
  • Learn to pay attention to reading and understand what you read
  • Learn whatever you want them to learn

What can you do with Indigo?

Create automated tests with multiple responses and random positioning of responses using custom images or images of the child's real environment with which he or she is familiar. You can also configure question attempts and count successes and failures.

Create automated comprehensive reading exercises with image identification and hit counting using personalized images or images of the child's real environment with which he or she is familiar.

Create stories using personalized images or images of the child's real environment with which he or she is familiar.

And if that weren't enough, Indigo is the ideal tool for learning or teaching any language.

Who can use Indigo?

Indigo is versatile and intuitive and can be used with children, young people or adults of any age even if they cannot read.
